# Content Expansion Roadmap

**GitaWisdom Scenario Database Growth Strategy**
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** November 15, 2025
**Timeline:** 16-week phased implementation

---

## Executive Summary

This roadmap outlines a **16-week plan** to expand the GitaWisdom scenario database from 1,225 to 1,325+ scenarios, adding **100+ high-priority scenarios** that address critical content gaps.

**Key Objectives:**
1. Add life-saving crisis scenarios (mental health, safety)
2. Expand severely underrepresented Financial category (15 → 30+ scenarios)
3. Include marginalized and underrepresented experiences
4. Balance chapter distribution (reduce Chapter 2 overuse)
5. Establish quality standards and review processes

**Success Metrics:**
- 100+ new scenarios published
- 70% reduction in zero-result searches
- Mental health professional approval
- User satisfaction > 4.5/5 for new scenarios
- Financial category doubled (15 → 30+)

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks & Mitigation Strategies

**Risk 1: Quality Degradation Under Speed Pressure**
- **Mitigation:** Mandatory quality checklist for each scenario
- **Mitigation:** Peer review process
- **Mitigation:** Expert review for crisis scenarios
- **Trigger:** If quality audit finds >10% failing checklist, slow down

**Risk 2: Insufficient Expert Review Availability**
- **Mitigation:** Build network of 3-5 reviewers per specialty
- **Mitigation:** Compensate experts fairly for time
- **Mitigation:** Stagger scenarios to avoid review bottlenecks

**Risk 3: Cultural Insensitivity or Misrepresentation**
- **Mitigation:** Consult with community advocates
- **Mitigation:** Hire sensitivity readers for marginalized experiences
- **Mitigation:** Beta test with diverse user groups

**Risk 4: Resource Link Outdatedness**
- **Mitigation:** Verify all hotlines/websites before publishing
- **Mitigation:** Quarterly review of crisis resources
- **Mitigation:** Automated link checking

**Risk 5: Writer Burnout on Difficult Topics**
- **Mitigation:** Rotate writers across crisis and non-crisis scenarios
- **Mitigation:** Provide mental health support for content creators
- **Mitigation:** Limit crisis scenarios to 2-3 per week per writer
