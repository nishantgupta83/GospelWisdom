# New Scenario Recommendations for GitaWisdom

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** November 15, 2025
**Based on:** Comprehensive database gap analysis of 1,225 existing scenarios

---

## Executive Summary

This document recommends **100+ new scenarios** to address critical content gaps identified in the GitaWisdom database. Scenarios are organized into:

1. **Tier 1 (URGENT):** 20 crisis and safety scenarios - **Implement immediately**
2. **Tier 2 (HIGH PRIORITY):** 30 severe hardship scenarios - **Implement next**
3. **Tier 3 (IMPORTANT):** 50+ underrepresented experience scenarios - **Phase 2 expansion**

Total recommendations: **100+ scenarios** addressing 69 high-priority gaps and 9 medium-priority gaps.

## Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Crisis Scenarios (Weeks 1-4)

**Week 1: Crisis Infrastructure**
- Create crisis resource database (hotlines, support groups, professional help)
- Develop trauma-informed language guidelines
- Consult with mental health professionals for sensitivity review

**Week 2-3: Write Tier 1 Crisis Scenarios (20 scenarios)**
- Priority order: U1, U14, U9, U3, U10 (top 5 CRITICAL)
- Include all required crisis resources
- Sensitivity review by professionals

**Week 4: Testing & Refinement**
- Beta test with mental health advocates
- Add trigger warnings where appropriate
- Finalize and publish

### Phase 2: High Priority Hardship (Weeks 5-8)

**Week 5-6: Financial Category Expansion (8 scenarios)**
- H9, H10, H11, H12, H13, H14 (bankruptcy to foreclosure)
- Include financial assistance resources
- Partner with financial counseling organizations

**Week 7-8: Chronic Health & Work Trauma (15 scenarios)**
- H1-H8 (chronic health)
- H17-H23 (work trauma)
- Medical accuracy review
- Include support group resources

### Phase 3: Underrepresented Experiences (Weeks 9-16)

**Week 9-10: Disability & Chronic Illness (10 scenarios)**
- I1-I10 (disability experiences)
- Consult disability advocates
- Ensure respectful, empowering language

**Week 11-12: Marginalized Communities (10 scenarios)**
- I11-I20 (refugee, LGBTQ+, racial minorities)
- Cultural sensitivity review
- Partner with community organizations

**Week 13-14: Mental Health Expansion (10 scenarios)**
- I21-I30 (bipolar, schizophrenia, anxiety disorders)
- Clinical accuracy review
- Include treatment resources

**Week 15-16: Modern Work/Family Issues (20 scenarios)**
- I31-I50 (imposter syndrome, blended families, etc.)
- Address contemporary challenges
- Validate modern struggles
